Project Overview

Preventing Maternal Deaths with AI

MamaGuardian AI is a predictive health analytics tool that uses supervised machine learning models trained on real maternal health data to identify high-risk pregnancies early.

This data-driven platform enables community health workers, clinics, and public hospitals to prioritize care, flag emergency cases, and optimize limited resources in Kenya.

Aligned with SDG 3 -- Good Health and Well-being, our project aims to lower maternal mortality rates through predictive intervention, especially among poor, rural, and marginalized women.
