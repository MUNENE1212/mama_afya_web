About MamaGuardian AI

MamaGuardian AI is a project dedicated to leveraging machine learning to reduce maternal deaths in Kenya. We target rural and underserved regions where access to maternal care is limited and preventable deaths are most common.

Our solution utilizes historical health records and real-time data to classify high-risk pregnancies, generate alerts for Community Health Volunteers (CHVs), and support county-level health decision-making through an intelligent dashboard.

Project Objectives

01. Predict High-Risk Pregnancies

Use machine learning to flag risk patterns early for timely medical attention.

02. Empower CHVs and Clinics

Provide community health workers with real-time insights via mobile interface.

03. Strengthen County Health Systems

Enable data-driven decisions at the county level with actionable dashboards.
