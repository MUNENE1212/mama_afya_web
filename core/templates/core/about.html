About Our Project

Understanding the maternal health crisis and our AI-powered solution

The Problem

Kenya's maternal mortality rate remains alarmingly high at 355 deaths per 100,000 live births, far exceeding the global SDG target of below 70 deaths per 100,000 live births by 2030.

Most maternal deaths in Kenya occur in rural and underserved areas where access to quality healthcare is limited. These deaths are largely preventable with proper early detection and intervention.

Without data-driven tools for early risk detection, healthcare workers are often forced to react to complications rather than prevent them.

Key Challenges

Delays in Accessing Healthcare

Many pregnant women face three critical delays: decision-making delays, transportation delays, and delays in receiving adequate care once at a facility.

Inadequate Health Infrastructure

Many rural clinics lack basic tools for maternal monitoring, such as ultrasound machines, blood pressure monitors, and fetal dopplers.

Lack of Predictive Data

Without digital tools and predictive analytics, early signs of complications are frequently overlooked until it's too late.

Our Solution

MamaGuardian AI is a smart, AI-driven maternal risk prediction system designed to identify high-risk pregnancies early, enabling timely medical intervention and reducing preventable maternal deaths in Kenya.

Our system uses supervised machine learning to analyze maternal health data and generate personalized risk assessments, ensuring that at-risk women receive priority care before complications escalate.

SDG Alignment

SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being

Our project directly contributes to Target 3.1: Reduce the global maternal mortality ratio to less than 70 per 100,000 live births.

By identifying high-risk pregnancies early, we enable health workers to act before complications become fatal, saving lives and improving maternal health outcomes.

The project also supports Kenya's Vision 2030 health pillar and the Linda Mama initiative by focusing on early detection and resource optimization.
